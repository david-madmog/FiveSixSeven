British Judo Association

Five Six Seven Judo club is affiliated to the British Judo Association.

All players must join the British Judo Association if they wish to continue doing judo

Membership of the organisation gives you:

  • Ability to earn grades
  • Ability to enter competitions
  • Public Liability and personal accident insurance
  • See the BJA website for more details

To join online, just Fill in the form on the BJA website
